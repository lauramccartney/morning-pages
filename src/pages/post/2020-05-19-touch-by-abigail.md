I touch and push you.\
Feel your elasticity.\
Release as I feel your body warming through my fingers.

\
You relinquish and bend.\
You forgive.\
You remember.\
I shape you.

A limit is reached. Collapse, bucket.\
A limit is reached.\
I look into you.\
You have touched me.
